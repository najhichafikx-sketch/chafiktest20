The Free plan gives you access to a limited number of generations (5 per day) and access to one primary tool so you can test the platform's capabilities before committing. No credit card is required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time right from your dashboard. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What exactly is the "Video to Prompt" tool? +
It's an advanced AI model that analyzes any video you upload and reverse-engineers it into highly detailed text prompts. This is perfect for recreating specific styles, lighting, or compositions in text-to-image or text-to-video AI generators.
Do I need technical skills to use Chafiktech Ai? +
Not at all! We've designed Chafiktech Ai to be incredibly intuitive and user-friendly. If you know how to copy and paste, you can start generating professional-grade content immediately.

Yes! API access is available on our Pro and Business plans. You can generate API keys directly from your dashboard to integrate our AI tools into your own applications or workflows.
